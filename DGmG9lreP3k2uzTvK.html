Design Principles

  • Use a stark black and white color scheme for maximum contrast
  • Incorporate a bright accent color for emphasis and calls-to-action

  • Use thick lines and borders for visual separation
  • Implement clear hover states for interactive elements
  • Maintain simplicity in layouts to enhance readability
