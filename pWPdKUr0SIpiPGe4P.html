Design Principles

  • Use a color palette inspired by beach elements: sand, ocean, coral, and seashells
  • Incorporate organic shapes and curved lines to mimic waves and natural beach forms
  • Utilize beach-related iconography such as palm trees, seashells, and waves
  • Implement a relaxed and casual typography with a mix of playful and clean fonts
  • Add subtle animations to simulate ocean waves or swaying palm trees
  • Use textures that evoke the feeling of sand, water, and beach materials
  • Create layouts that suggest openness and the expanse of the beach horizon
