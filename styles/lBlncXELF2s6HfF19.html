Design Principles

  • Use natural, earthy tones to reflect our connection with nature
  • Incorporate leaf and grass imagery subtly in design elements
  • Maintain a clean, modern look with ample white space
  • Ensure all text is easily readable with good contrast
  • Use rounded corners and organic shapes to soften the design

Image Guidelines

  • Use high-quality photographs of lush, well-maintained lawns and gardens
  • Include images of our team at work to showcase professionalism
  • Incorporate before-and-after images to demonstrate service quality
  • Ensure all images are properly compressed for web use
