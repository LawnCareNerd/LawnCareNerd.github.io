Design Principles

  • Use a color palette inspired by forest elements: deep greens, earthy browns, and misty grays
  • Incorporate organic shapes and textures to mimic tree bark, leaves, and forest undergrowth
  • Utilize forest-related iconography such as trees, woodland creatures, and leaves
  • Implement a mix of sturdy and elegant typography to reflect the strength and beauty of forests
  • Add subtle animations to simulate rustling leaves or gentle forest movements
  • Use textures that evoke the feeling of tree bark, moss, and forest floor materials
  • Create layouts that suggest the layered, vertical nature of forest environments
