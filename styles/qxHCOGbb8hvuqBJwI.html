Design Principles

  • Use typewriter-style fonts to mimic old telegram typography
  • Incorporate telegram-specific elements like stamps, borders, and header text
  • Utilize a limited color palette reminiscent of vintage paper and ink
  • Implement a structured layout with clear hierarchy
  • Use subtle textures or patterns to emulate aged paper
  • Include telegram-specific elements like date stamps, addresses, and word counts
  • Consider using all caps for headers and important information
