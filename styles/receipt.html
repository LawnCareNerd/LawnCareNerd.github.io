Design Principles

  • Use monospaced fonts to mimic typewriter or dot-matrix printer output
  • Incorporate dashed or perforated lines to simulate tear-off sections
  • Utilize a limited color palette reminiscent of thermal paper and ink
  • Implement a clean, structured layout with clear hierarchy
  • Use subtle textures or patterns to emulate paper grain
  • Include receipt-specific elements like item lists, totals, and transaction numbers
  • Consider print-friendly designs for actual receipt functionality
