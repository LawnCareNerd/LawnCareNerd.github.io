Design Principles

  • Use rich, warm colors inspired by jazz clubs and instruments
  • Incorporate musical elements and iconography
  • Create a sense of rhythm and movement in layouts
  • Use elegant typography with a mix of serif and sans-serif fonts
  • Add subtle animations to mimic the fluidity of jazz music
  • Include interactive elements that respond like musical instruments
  • Use curved lines and organic shapes to reflect the improvisational nature of jazz
