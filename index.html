Dev Log - LawnCareNerd.com

Welcome to the development log for LawnCareNerd.com. Here you'll find updates and notes on the ongoing development of the site:

August 7, 2024

Started working on the dev log page. Decided to use VT323 for headers and Courier Prime for the main text to give the page a unique, hand-crafted feel. The directory is also being expanded to include more sections as the site grows.

July 30, 2024

Began transitioning content from the old LawnCareNerd.github.io site to the new domain at LawnCareNerd.com. Focused on establishing a consistent style and integrating new features that cater to lawn care businesses.
