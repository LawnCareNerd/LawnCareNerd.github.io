Design Principles

  • Use bold, high-contrast colors reminiscent of safety equipment and heavy machinery
  • Incorporate industrial textures like metal, concrete, and caution stripes
  • Utilize strong, robust typography that conveys strength and durability
  • Include iconography related to construction and heavy equipment
  • Implement grid-based layouts to reflect precision and organization
  • Add subtle animations to simulate machinery or construction processes
  • Use angular shapes and bold lines to create a sense of power and stability
