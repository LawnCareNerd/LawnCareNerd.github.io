Design Principles

  • Use cool, crisp colors that evoke the feeling of a winter landscape
  • Incorporate elegant, serif typography for headings to suggest tradition and warmth
  • Utilize winter-related iconography such as snowflakes, evergreen trees, and icicles
  • Implement gentle animations to simulate falling snow or twinkling lights
  • Create layouts that suggest coziness and protection from the cold
  • Use gradients and textures to mimic the look of ice and frost
  • Integrate nature-inspired patterns like knit textures or snowflake designs
